Upload Brain Image Data

In order to upload your confocal microscopy images to the Brain Image Library (BIL), the following steps need to be completed:

  • Create an account on the BIL's metadata submission site.
  • This will trigger a process to begin creating an account on the BIL's HPC storage and compute resource.
  • You will receive an email from grants@psc.edu telling you to create a password at apr.psc.edu
  • Once your account on BIL's HPC storage and compute resource is created, you can request staging area where your image data can be uploaded.
