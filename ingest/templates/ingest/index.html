Overview

The Brain Image Library (BIL) is a national public resource enabling researchers to deposit, analyze, mine, share and interact with large brain image datasets. The BIL is supported by the National Institute of Mental Health of the National Institutes of Health under award number R24MH114793.

Collections

Imaging datasets in the Brain Image Library have metadata associated with them. This metadata includes everything from experimental information to the layout of the files. The combination of metadata and datasets make up what we call a collection. Collections are ultimately what gets submitted for publication in the BIL and made publically available to the research community.
